SwipeRight provides general information about credit card rewards programs for educational purposes only. This is not financial advice.

  • Annual fees, earn rates, and card benefits change frequently without notice. Always verify current terms at the issuer's official website before making any financial decisions.
  • Merchant category codes (MCCs) vary by location and processor. A store that earns 4x today may code differently at a different branch or after an issuer update.
  • Rotating categories (Discover IT, Chase Freedom Flex, US Bank Cash+) require quarterly activation. Confirm you've activated before relying on bonus rates.
  • Card eligibility, credit limits, and approval are determined solely by the issuing bank based on your creditworthiness.
  • SwipeRight is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by any credit card issuer or bank.
